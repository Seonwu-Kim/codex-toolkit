# Incident Report Outline

## 1. 장애 요약

State incident ID, severity, status, start/end time, duration, affected
services, and a concise factual summary.

## 2. 영향도

Quantify affected users/requests, unavailable or degraded functions, data
impact, geographic scope, financial impact when known, and confidence level.

## 3. 탐지 및 대응

Explain the first signal, alert path, escalation, mitigation, recovery, and
communication.

## 4. 타임라인

Use absolute timestamp, event, actor/team, evidence source, and significance.

## 5. 원인 분석

Separate triggering event, root cause, contributing factors, and safeguards
that failed or were absent. Mark inference and unresolved questions.

## 6. 조치 내역

Record immediate mitigation and recovery actions with result and evidence.

## 7. 재발 방지 작업

Use action ID, corrective action, owner, priority, due date, status, and
verification method.

## 8. 교훈 및 후속 검증

Capture what worked, what did not, review date, and criteria for closing the
incident.
