# Meeting Notes Outline

## 1. 회의 개요

Record meeting title, date/time with timezone, location/channel, facilitator,
recorder, attendees, absentees when relevant, and purpose.

## 2. 핵심 요약

Summarize the outcome in three to five bullets.

## 3. 결정 사항

Use decision ID, decision, rationale, effective date, and approver/owner.

## 4. 액션 아이템

Use action ID, task, owner, due date, priority, dependency, and completion
criterion.

## 5. 미해결 질문

Record question, decision owner, required evidence, and target resolution date.

## 6. 다음 회의

Record the date, purpose, and prerequisites only when scheduled.
